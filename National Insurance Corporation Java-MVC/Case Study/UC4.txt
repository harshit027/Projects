UC4-Make Policy 

Level: User goal

Scope: National Insurance Company

Primary Actor: Acc Clerk

Stakeholders and interests:
• Customer:
o Wants to recieve payment reciept and policy Id.
• Clerk:
o Wants the system to accurately register the customer details and generate policy id and reciept.
• NIC:
o Wants to make claim registration and generation process fast and reliable.

Preconditions:
• Customer details have been forwarded by P&C 
• Customer has made payment.

Success Guarantee: Customer's policy is registered and policy id and reciept are generated.

Main Success Scenario:
1. Customer goes to Account Dept to collect reciept.
2. System validates the customer details
3. Clerk registers the customer details into the system.
4. System generates reciept and policy id.

Extensions:
2a. Invalid Customer Details
1. System displays an error message.
2. System displays menu.
