UC3-Update Claim 

Level: User goal

Scope: National Insurance Company

Primary Actor: P & C Clerk

Stakeholders and interests:
• Clerk:
o Wants the system to accurately update the claim validity details .
• NIC:
o Wants to make updation process fast and reliable.

Preconditions:
• The claim should exist in the system.

Success Guarantee: Validation details are updated in the system. 

Main Success Scenario:
1. Clerk enters the updation details into the system.
2. System validates the updation details.
3. System records the updated details.


Extensions:
2a. Invalid Updation Details
1. System displays an error message.
2. System displays menu.
