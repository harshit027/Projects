UC1-Add Details


Level: User goal

Scope: National Insurance Company
Primary Actor: P & C Clerk

Stakeholders and interests:

• Customer:
o Wants to register and create policy with NIC . 
• Clerk:
o Wants the system to accurately record the details provided by the customer to register
with the company.
• NIC:
o Wants to make customer registration process fast and reliable.

Preconditions:
• none

Success Guarantee: Customer details given by the customer is recorded. 

Main Success Scenario:
1. Customer arrives at NIC and provides details for registration.
2. Clerk enters the details into the system
3. System validates the customer details.
4. System forwards details to accounts dept.

Extensions:
3a. Invalid Customer Details
1. System displays an error message.
2. System displays menu.
